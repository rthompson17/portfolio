I'm in the process of building a marketing website for the musician, CAVE DIVER, who also owns and manages a recording studio called Spacejam.

The site will initially be a basic frontend website as a minimum viable product, but I'll eventually refactor using a MERN stack as a way of creating a CMS for the client. They'll then be able to make updates to the client gallery and audio samples featured on the site over time.
